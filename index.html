Tic-Tac-Toe!

Tic-Tac-Toe is a game known to most, if not all, people in the United States. Sometimes reffered to as noughts and crosses in the UK, this game involves taking turns placing an 'X' or an 'O' on a three-by-three grid. The objective is to get "three-in-a-row", which can be vertical, horizontal, or diagonal.
